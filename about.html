About Us

The roots of education are bitter,
But the fruit is sweet

UKF College of Engineering & Technology, established in 2009 and managed by the Universal Knowledge Foundation Trust, has steadily evolved into a distinguished institution in the field of professional education. Over the years, the college has built a strong reputation for academic excellence, innovation, and holistic student development.

With state-of-the-art infrastructure and a team of highly qualified, experienced, and dedicated faculty members, UKF stands as a center of knowledge, growth, and inspiration. We take pride in the healthy relationships nurtured among students, staff, and management, which, combined with continuously upgraded facilities, create an ideal environment for learning and research.

The consistent academic achievements of our students reflect the institution’s commitment to excellence, while their success in co-curricular and extracurricular activities highlights the emphasis placed on all-round development. At UKF, education goes beyond the classroom — students are equipped with essential life skills through expert-led sessions in communication, soft skills, and career readiness. Strong industry linkages further provide valuable exposure, enabling students to gain practical insights into professional environments and make informed career choices.

UKF College of Engineering & Technology is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi, to conduct:

  • 4-year (8-semester) B.Tech degree programmes
  • 2-year (4-semester) M.Tech degree programmes

Undergraduate Programmes (B.Tech – 4 Years, 8 Semesters)

  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical & Electronics Engineering
  • Electronics & Communication Engineering
  • Computer Science & Engineering

Postgraduate Programmes (M.Tech – 2 Years, 4 Semesters)

  • Electrical Power Systems
  • Environmental Engineering & Management

Our Leadership Team

The Universal Knowledge Foundation, a trust registered under the Indian Trust Act in 2008, was established with the noble objective of providing dedicated service to the cause of higher education in the field of Engineering and Technology.
Under the visionary leadership of Dr. S. Basant, Chairman of UKF, the institution is steadily creating a strong presence in the professional education sector of the state. With a focus on academic excellence, innovation, and values, the Trust is committed to shaping competent and dynamic technocrats who are not only industry-ready but also equipped to contribute meaningfully to the progress and development of our nation in this era of rapid technological advancement.

Dr. S. Basant

Chairman, UKF College of Engineering & Technology

Managing Director, Kshema Wind Farm Services (P) Ltd. | Gee Bee Hotels & Resorts (P) Ltd.

Dr. S. Basant, born on 19 February 1963 in Perumbavoor, Ernakulam District, is a distinguished educator, entrepreneur, and pioneer in the renewable energy sector. He began his professional journey as a teacher in 1981 and soon expanded his vision to the field of renewable energy, at a time when wind power was emerging in India.

In 1987, he founded Kshema Wind Farm Services (P) Ltd., which went on to achieve the historic milestone of installing the first windmill in Kerala at Ramakkalmedu, Idukki District. Under his leadership, the company has successfully installed windmills across Kerala and Tamil Nadu, contributing to a combined generation capacity of over 2000 MW.

Beyond renewable energy, Dr. Basant serves as the Managing Director of Gee Bee Hotels & Resorts (P) Ltd., Angamaly, and as the Chairman of UKF College of Engineering & Technology, Parippally, where his commitment to education and innovation continues to inspire future generations.

Dr. Basant is married to Smt. Lovely Basant, a teacher at the Government Girls’ Higher Secondary School, Perumbavoor.

They are blessed with two children:

  • Smt. Amritha Basanth – General Secretary, UKF College of Engineering & Technology, married to Dr. Prasobh K. Prabha.
  • Mr. Abhinand Basant – Director, Arsha Kerala Ayurveda Medical Innovations (AKAMI), Angamaly; currently pursuing higher studies at The University of Manchester, UK.

Awards & Recognitions

  • Best Renewable Energy Developer Award (2016) – Conferred on 23 October 2016 in Madhya Pradesh by Mr. Upendra Tripathi, Secretary, MNRE, in the presence of senior officials from Renewable Energy and NTPC.
  • Brand Icon 2016 – Social Enterprises – Awarded by The Times of India, Kerala on 29 July 2016.
  • Doctor of Industry (2013) – Conferred by Colombo University for his outstanding contributions to renewable energy.

Vision & Mission

Vision

UKFCET envisions becoming a global hub of excellence in engineering education and research, shaping students into skilled professionals through a culture of critical thinking, creativity, innovation, entrepreneurship, and a strong sense of social responsibility.

Mission

UKFCET empowers its student engineers with the skills to innovate for both work and life, fostering strong partnerships with industry and society to build a sustainable future.

Quality Policy

We are dedicated to shaping young professionals through a high-quality, consistently improving education system, supported by state-of-the-art infrastructure and student services. Our approach fosters an entrepreneurial mindset, nurturing a generation that is globally competent and socially responsible.
